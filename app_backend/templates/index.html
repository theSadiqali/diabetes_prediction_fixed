Diabetes Prediction & Chatbot

Dataset Note

This project was trained on a 700-record Figshare diabetes dataset. It uses the following features (columns):

  • Pregnancies — Number of pregnancies
  • Glucose — Plasma glucose concentration
  • BloodPressure — Diastolic blood pressure
  • SkinThickness — Triceps skin fold thickness
  • Insulin — Serum insulin (mu U/ml)
  • BMI — Body Mass Index
  • DiabetesPedigreeFunction — Diabetes heritage score
  • Age — Age of the patient
  • Outcome — Diabetes presence (1 = Yes, 0 = No)
